December 29, 2010
Volume 132, Issue 51
Pages 17977-18430
About the Cover:

When the click-chemistry, tight-binding TZ2PA6 inhibitors are co-crystallized with acetylcholinesterase, time-dependent conformational remodeling of the crystalline complexes is observed that correlates with the dissociation half-times of the solution complexes. Hence, the initial complexes kinetically driven in solution slowly form more stable complexes governed by thermodynamic equilibrium and observable in mature crystals. See Marchot, Bourne, and co-workers, p 18292. View the article.
