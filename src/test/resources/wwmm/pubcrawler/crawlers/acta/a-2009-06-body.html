Experimental charge-density study of paracetamol - multipole refinement in the presence of a disordered methyl group

J. M. Bak, P. M. Dominiak, C. C. Wilson and K. Wozniak

Synopsis: On the basis of high-resolution single-crystal X-ray diffraction data for paracetamol, different approaches (including those based on pseudoatom databases) to modeling of the static experimental electron density in the presence of a dynamically disordered molecular fragment were tested. The electrostatic properties obtained were compared with the results of theoretical single-point periodic calculations.

The OD interpretation of the crystal structure of kettnerite CaBiOFCO3

J. Hybler and S. Durovic

Synopsis: The mineral kettnerite, CaBi(OFCO3), has an order-disorder (OD) structure with a quadratic net built up by more than one kind of layer. Two maximum-degree-of-order (MDO) (1O, 2O) and one non-MDO (2Q) polytypes are derived by stacking of regularly alternating polar OD packets instead of layers. The stacking rule allows the existence of out-of-step, twin and upside-down domains.
